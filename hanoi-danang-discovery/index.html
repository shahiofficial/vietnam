Day by day

Your itinerary

DAY 01

Arrival in Hanoi – Old Quarter & Night Market

Hanoi

Arrive at Noi Bai International Airport, Hanoi. Private airport pick-up and transfer to your 3★ hotel for check-in and rest.

Evening – Hanoi Old Quarter:

  • Walk around the legendary Hoan Kiem Lake
  • Discover the vibrant Old Quarter and its 36 trade streets
  • Explore Dong Xuan Night Market
🍽  Meals: None
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 02

Train Street, Incense Village & Halong Bay Cruise

Hanoi / Halong Bay

Breakfast at the hotel.

Morning – local Hanoi experiences:

  • Visit the famous Train Street — sip Vietnamese coffee as the train rolls by
  • Trip to the colourful Quang Phu Cau Incense Village — vibrant red and yellow incense fields

Full-day Halong Bay Cruise:

  • Private transfer to Halong Bay (included)
  • Sail through thousands of dramatic limestone karsts
  • Visit a stunning cave such as Sung Sot (Surprise) Cave
  • Kayaking experience
  • Lunch included on board

Return to Hanoi in the evening.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 03

Ninh Binh Day Trip

Ninh Binh

Breakfast at the hotel. 08:00 — Pick-up for a full-day Ninh Binh tour (approx. 2 hours' drive south).

Highlights of the day:

  • Boat trip through the river caves of Trang An or Tam Coc
  • Climb to Mua Cave viewpoint for panoramic dragon-spine landscape views
  • Lunch with local Ninh Binh specialties

Return to Hanoi by late afternoon.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 04

Hanoi → Da Nang – Dragon Bridge & Beach

Hanoi → Da Nang

Breakfast and check out. Private transfer to the airport for your domestic flight to Da Nang. On arrival, private transfer to your 3★ hotel for check-in.

Evening – Da Nang highlights:

  • Visit the iconic Dragon Bridge
  • Stroll along Da Nang's beautiful My Khe Beach
  • Optional: dinner at a beachfront restaurant
🍽  Meals: Breakfast
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Da Nang.
DAY 05

Ba Na Hills OR Hoi An Tour

Da Nang

Breakfast at the hotel. Full-day tour — choose one of two options:

Option 1 – Ba Na Hills:

  • Ride the record-breaking Ba Na Hills cable car
  • Visit the world-famous Golden Bridge held by giant stone hands
  • Explore the French Village with European-style architecture
  • Le Jardin D'Amour flower gardens and Fantasy Park

Option 2 – Hoi An Ancient Town:

  • Wander Hoi An's UNESCO World Heritage old town
  • Visit the Japanese Covered Bridge, ancient houses and assembly halls
  • Try a traditional basket-boat ride in the coconut grove
  • Witness Hoi An's magical lantern-lit evenings

Return to your Da Nang hotel in the evening.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Da Nang.
DAY 06

Departure

Da Nang

Breakfast, packing and check out. Free time until your private transfer to Da Nang International Airport for your departure flight.

End of services.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast
