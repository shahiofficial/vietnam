DAY 01

Hanoi Arrival – Welcome to Vietnam

Hanoi

Arrival at Noi Bai International Airport, Hanoi. Welcome by our driver and transfer to your 3★ hotel in the Old Quarter.

After check-in, enjoy free time to relax after your flight. Depending on your arrival time, you may explore:

  • Hoan Kiem Lake – the legendary heart of Hanoi
  • Hanoi Old Quarter with its 36 trade streets
  • Vietnamese street food experience
  • Optional: traditional Water Puppet Show
🍽  Meals: None
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 02

Hanoi → Ninh Binh → Overnight Train to Sapa

Ninh Binh → Hanoi → Sapa

Breakfast at the hotel.

Depart for Ninh Binh – one of the most beautiful countryside destinations in Northern Vietnam.

Highlights of the day:

  • Hoa Lu Ancient Capital – former capital of Vietnam
  • Boat trip through Trang An or Tam Coc – limestone mountains, peaceful rivers and rice fields
  • Lunch at a local restaurant
  • Climb to Mua Cave viewpoint for panoramic karst landscape views

Late afternoon – return to Hanoi around 7:00 PM. One free shower and change is provided at the hotel before going to the train.

10:00 PM – board the overnight A/C semi-sleeper train to Sapa.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight on A/C semi-sleeper train.
DAY 03

Sapa Arrival – Lao Chai & Ta Van Trekking

Sapa / Muong Hoa Valley

Early morning arrival at Lao Cai station. Bus transfer to Sapa. Arrive at hotel, freshen up and have breakfast.

9:00 AM – start the trekking tour to ethnic villages:

  • Trek through Muong Hoa Valley with stunning rice terraces
  • Visit Lao Chai village – home of the Black H'mong people
  • Continue to Ta Van village of the Dzay minority people
  • Lunch with a local family – learn about traditional architecture and customs

Afternoon return to Sapa town. Free time in the evening to explore Sapa night market for shopping and local ethnic activities.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Sapa.
DAY 04

Rong May Glass Bridge – Mong Alpine Coaster – Sun Plaza

Sapa

Breakfast at the hotel.

Morning – Rong May Glass Bridge & Sky Walk:

  • Transfer to Rong May Glass Bridge – one of the most exciting attractions near Sapa
  • Take the lift up the cliff and experience the thrilling Sky Bridge Walk
  • Panoramic mountain views from the glass-floor platform
  • Photo opportunities with O Quy Ho Pass in the background

Afternoon – Mong Village & Alpine Coaster:

  • Visit Mong Village in the Sapa highlands
  • Enjoy the thrilling Alpine Coaster ride through the valley
  • Scenic photo stops along the way

Evening – Sun Plaza Sapa:

  • Visit the iconic Sun Plaza – Sapa's European-style landmark
  • Coffee with mountain views and shopping at the plaza
  • Free evening to explore Sapa town
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Sapa.
DAY 05

Fansipan Peak – Cat Cat Village – Semi-sleeper Bus to Hanoi

Sapa → Hanoi

Breakfast at the hotel.

Morning – Fansipan Cable Car experience:

  • Transfer to Sun World Fansipan Legend area
  • Take the cable car to Fansipan Peak – the 'Roof of Indochina'
  • Breathtaking views of the Hoang Lien Son mountain range
  • Walk up 600 stairs to Fansipan's peak
  • Visit mountaintop pagodas at 2,800 metres

Afternoon – Cat Cat Village & Moana View:

  • Visit Cat Cat Village – home of the Black H'mong people
  • Traditional handicraft workshops and Cat Cat waterfall
  • Moana View – popular check-in spot with panoramic terraces

Evening – last-minute Sapa Market shopping and check out from the hotel.

Night – board the luxury limousine semi-sleeper bus from Sapa to Hanoi.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight on luxury limousine semi-sleeper bus.
DAY 06

Halong Bay Overnight Cruise

Halong Bay

Early morning arrival in Hanoi. Direct transfer to Halong Bay (approx. 2.5 hours).

Arrival at the cruise port. Check-in procedures and board your overnight cruise. Welcome drink on board.

Cruise highlights:

  • Sail through the magnificent limestone islands of Halong Bay
  • Lunch served on board
  • Visit Sung Sot (Surprise) Cave – the largest cave in Halong Bay
  • Stop at Ti Top Island – climb to the panoramic viewpoint, swim from the beach
  • Explore Luon Cave by kayak or bamboo boat
  • Relax on the sundeck during sunset
  • Dinner on board with Vietnamese cuisine
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight on Halong Bay cruise in a private cabin with ensuite bathroom.
DAY 07

Cruise Check-Out – Departure

Halong Bay → Hanoi → Airport

Sunrise on Halong Bay. Light breakfast on board.

Continue cruising before brunch and disembarkation.

Transfer from Halong Bay directly to Noi Bai International Airport for your departure flight.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Brunch
End of services.
