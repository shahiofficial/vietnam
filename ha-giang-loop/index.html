Day by day

Your itinerary

DAY 01

Hanoi Arrival – City Tour – Train to Sapa

Hanoi → Sapa

Arrive at Hanoi Airport. Pick-up and transfer to our office to leave your luggage.

12:30 PM – 5:00 PM — Half-day city tour, 'Oriental Discovering Hanoi Inside':

  • Hoan Kiem Lake & Ngoc Son Temple
  • Hanoi Old Quarter with its 36 trade streets
  • Temple of Literature — Vietnam's first national university
  • Local Vietnamese coffee experience

Dinner at a local restaurant.

9:00 PM — Pick-up for the train station. Board the A/C 4-berth sleeping train from Hanoi to Sapa.

🍽  Meals: Lunch, Dinner
Overnight on the sleeper train.
DAY 02

Lao Cai – Sapa – Lao Chai – Ta Van Village

Sapa / Muong Hoa Valley

5:30 AM — Arrive at Lao Cai station. Bus pick-up to our office in Sapa. Shower and breakfast, then 9:00 AM start the trekking tour to ethnic minority villages.

Trekking highlights:

  • Walk through Y Linh Ho village — home of the Black H'mong people
  • Continue to Lao Chai village through terraced rice fields
  • End at Ta Van village of the Dzay minority people
  • Lunch with a local family

Around 4:00 PM — finish the trek and return to your Sapa hotel.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at hotel in Sapa.
DAY 03

Fansipan Peak – Sleeper Bus to Ha Giang

Sapa → Ha Giang

Breakfast at the hotel.

8:00 AM – 2:00 PM — Visit Fansipan Peak:

  • Take the cable car to ascend to 2,800 metres
  • Walk up the 600 steps to Fansipan's peak — the 'Roof of Indochina'
  • Visit the mountaintop pagodas and viewpoints

Lunch in Sapa town. Board the Limousine / Sleeping Bus at 4:00 PM or 7:00 PM from Sapa to Ha Giang.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight on bus / arrive Ha Giang.
DAY 04

Ha Giang Loop – Easy Rider to Dong Van

Ha Giang → Dong Van (135 km)

Begin your Ha Giang Loop adventure by Easy Rider (motorbike with an experienced local rider). Route: Ha Giang City → Quan Ba → Yen Minh → Pho Cao → Sung La → Dong Van Town.

Highlights of the day:

  • Quan Ba — the famous 'Heaven's Gate' viewpoint and Twin Mountains
  • Yen Minh — pine forest valleys and scenic mountain roads
  • Pho Cao & Sung La — H'mong villages and stone houses
  • Arrive at Dong Van town in the evening
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Stay at homestay in Dong Van town.
DAY 05

Ma Pi Leng Pass – Nho Que River – Du Gia

Dong Van → Du Gia

Full day Easy Rider tour from Dong Van through Vietnam's most spectacular landscapes.

Highlights of the day:

  • Ma Pi Leng Pass — one of the 'Four Great Mountain Passes' of Vietnam
  • Nho Que River — emerald-green river winding through limestone canyons
  • Mau Due — scenic mountain views and ethnic villages
  • Du Gia waterfall — refreshing natural pools
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Stay at homestay in Du Gia.
DAY 06

Du Gia – Ha Giang – Hanoi

Du Gia → Hanoi

After breakfast, walk around the village and bathe in Du Gia waterfall. Depart back to Ha Giang City, with stops at:

  • Duong Thuong Valley — scenic photo stops
  • Lung Tam weaving village — traditional H'mong textile crafts
  • Lunch at Tam Son town / Quan Ba

Return to Ha Giang City around 4:00 PM. Shower and rest at the hostel / homestay.

Take the sleeper A/C cabin luxury bus to Hanoi:

  • 4:00 PM — Limousine bus → arrives Hanoi 10:00 PM
  • 6:00 PM — Sleeper bus → arrives Hanoi 12:00 AM
  • 8:30 PM — VIP cabin bus → arrives Hanoi 3:00 AM
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight on sleeper A/C cabin luxury bus.
