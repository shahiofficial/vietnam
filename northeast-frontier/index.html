Hanoi Departure
DAY 01

Arrive Hanoi

Hanoi

Arrive at Hanoi airport and transfer to your hotel. Free and easy to discover the Old Quarter and Hoan Kiem Lake.

🍽  Meals: None
Overnight at hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 02

Hanoi → Ha Giang

Hanoi → Ha Giang

Breakfast and check out. Around 9:00 AM board the bus from Hanoi to Ha Giang, arriving Ha Giang city around 4:00 PM. Check in to your homestay and explore Ha Giang's night life.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast
Overnight at homestay in Ha Giang.
DAY 03

Ha Giang → Quan Ba → Yen Minh

Quan Ba / Yen Minh

After breakfast, begin the legendary Ha Giang Loop.

Today's highlights:

  • KM 0 Ha Giang marker
  • Bac Sum Pass & Quan Ba Heaven Gate
  • Quan Ba Twin Mountains & Tam Son Town
  • Lung Khuy Cave, lunch in Tam Son
  • Can Ty Pass, lonely trees & Van Chai cliff
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at homestay in Yen Minh.
DAY 04

Yen Minh → Lung Cu → Dong Van

Lung Cu / Dong Van

Depart for Tham Ma Pass, Pho Cao village and Sung La valley.

Today's highlights:

  • Tham Ma Pass & Sung La valley
  • Vuong family mansion (King of the H'mong)
  • Lung Cu Flag Pole – the northernmost point of Vietnam
  • Lo Lo Chai cultural village
  • Dong Van old town & night market
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at hotel/homestay in Dong Van.
DAY 05

Ma Pi Leng → Nho Que River → Du Gia

Meo Vac / Du Gia

Ride the legendary Happiness Road and the majestic Ma Pi Leng Pass high above the Nho Que River.

Today's highlights:

  • Ma Pi Leng Pass & Sky Path panorama
  • Boat on the Nho Que River through Tu San Canyon – the deepest in Southeast Asia
  • Lunch at Meo Vac, scenic ride to Du Gia via Mau Due and Lung Ho
  • Tay homestay dinner with corn wine ('happy water')
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at homestay in Du Gia.
DAY 06

Du Gia → Lung Tam → Ha Giang

Quan Ba / Ha Giang

Walk around the village and swim at Du Gia Waterfall, then ride back toward Ha Giang city.

On the way:

  • Du Gia Waterfall
  • Duong Thuong valley
  • Lung Tam weaving village
  • Lunch at Tam Son – Quan Ba, arrive Ha Giang ~4 PM
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at homestay in Ha Giang.
DAY 07

Ha Giang → Me Pia 14 Corners → Pac Bo

Bao Lac / Cao Bang

Breakfast, then depart for Cao Bang province via Xuan Truong and Bao Lam.

Today's highlights:

  • Me Pia Pass & its famous 14 Corners (a short 1 km climb for the best view)
  • Lenin Stream
  • Pac Bo Cave
  • Tay cultural village stay in Pac Bo (separate bungalows)
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at Tay homestay in Pac Bo.
DAY 08

Pac Bo → Trung Khanh → Ban Gioc Waterfall

Tra Linh / Ban Gioc

Ride to Ban Gioc Waterfall via Tra Linh and Trung Khanh.

Today's highlights:

  • Lunch near Ban Gioc
  • Ban Gioc Waterfall – the largest waterfall on the Vietnam–China border
  • Bamboo-raft boat to the foot of the falls
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at homestay near Ban Gioc.
DAY 09

Ban Gioc → Nguom Ngao Cave → Cao Bang → Hanoi

Cao Bang → Hanoi

Visit Nguom Ngao Cave and explore Trung Khanh and Tra Linh.

Today's highlights:

  • Nguom Ngao Cave
  • Angel Eye Mountain (Mat Than)
  • Lunch in Cao Bang city
  • ~2:00 PM limousine bus from Cao Bang, arriving Hanoi ~8:00 PM
  • Check in to your Hanoi hotel
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight at hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 10

Hanoi Departure

Hanoi → Airport

Breakfast and last-minute shopping, then transfer to Hanoi International Airport. End of tour.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast
End of services.
