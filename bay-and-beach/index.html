DAY 01

Arrival in Hanoi – City Welcome

Hanoi

Arrive in Hanoi and transfer to your hotel in the Old Quarter.

Welcome lunch, then a half-day city tour – or spend the day at leisure with a gentle orientation around the city.

Highlights:

  • Hoan Kiem Lake – the heart of Hanoi
  • Hanoi Old Quarter & its 36 trade streets
  • Hoa Lo (Hilton) Prison
  • Temple of Literature
  • Local train-street cafés
  • Evening street life
🍽  Meals: Lunch
Overnight at hotel in Hanoi Old Quarter.
DAY 02

Hanoi – Ninh Binh Day Trip – Hanoi

Hanoi → Ninh Binh → Hanoi

Travel to Ninh Binh, often called the 'Ha Long Bay on Land' for its river landscapes, rice fields and limestone mountains. A sampan boat ride through Tam Coc or Trang An is one of the most scenic experiences in Northern Vietnam.

Highlights of the day:

  • Visit Hoa Lu Ancient Capital
  • Sampan boat trip through Trang An – limestone karsts and caves
  • Peaceful countryside views
  • Optional cycling around villages and rice fields
  • Optional climb to Hang Mua viewpoint for panoramic scenery
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight at hotel in Hanoi Old Quarter.
DAY 03

Hanoi – Ha Long Bay Overnight Cruise

Hanoi → Ha Long Bay

Depart Hanoi for Ha Long Bay and board your overnight cruise – emerald waters, limestone islands, caves, floating communities, kayaking and sunset views from the sundeck.

On board today:

  • Scenic transfer from Hanoi to Ha Long Bay
  • Welcome lunch on board
  • Cruise among limestone islands and islets
  • Visit a cave or island (depending on the cruise program)
  • Kayaking or bamboo boat activity
  • Sunset on the sundeck
  • Dinner and overnight on the cruise
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight on Ha Long Bay cruise in a private cabin.
DAY 04

Ha Long Bay – Hanoi – Fly to Phu Quoc

Ha Long Bay → Hanoi → Phu Quoc

Wake early for sunrise over Ha Long Bay. Join a light morning activity such as Tai Chi, enjoy breakfast, then continue cruising before disembarkation. Transfer back toward Hanoi for your domestic flight to Phu Quoc Island.

Today's journey:

  • Sunrise on Ha Long Bay
  • Morning cruise experience
  • Brunch on board
  • Transfer to airport
  • Domestic flight to Phu Quoc
  • Check in to your beach resort
  • Free evening at leisure
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Brunch
Overnight at hotel in Phu Quoc.
DAY 05

Phu Quoc Island – Optional Tours

Phu Quoc

A free day on Phu Quoc Island to relax on the beach – or add an optional tour.

Optional tours (on request):

  • Safari tour
  • Grand World tour
  • 3-islands tour
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight at hotel in Phu Quoc.
DAY 06

Phu Quoc Departure

Phu Quoc → Airport

Enjoy breakfast and free time until your airport transfer. End of your 6 days / 5 nights Vietnam journey.

Before you go:

  • Last-minute shopping or coffee
  • Transfer to Phu Quoc airport
🍽  Meals: Breakfast
