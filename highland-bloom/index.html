Day by day

Your itinerary

DAY 01

Arrival in Hanoi – Old Quarter

Hanoi

Arrive at Noi Bai International Airport, Hanoi. Private airport pick-up and transfer to your 3★ hotel in the Old Quarter for check-in and rest.

Hanoi sightseeing:

  • Visit the legendary Hoan Kiem Lake
  • Explore Ngoc Son Temple on the lake's small island
  • Shopping at Dong Xuan Market — the biggest wholesale market in Hanoi
  • Evening walk around the vibrant Old Quarter
🍽  Meals: None
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 02

Hanoi → Halong Bay Overnight Cruise

Halong Bay

Breakfast and check out. Pick-up and transfer to Halong Bay for a 2 Days / 1 Night cruise.

Cruise highlights:

  • Sail through thousands of dramatic limestone karsts
  • Visit a stunning cave inside the bay
  • Kayaking experience
  • Swimming and relaxing on the sundeck
  • Lunch and dinner served on board
🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight on the cruise in a private cabin with ensuite bathroom.
DAY 03

Halong Bay → Hanoi → Da Lat

Halong Bay → Hanoi → Da Lat

Early-morning activities on board as per the cruise itinerary. Early check out and bus transfer back to Hanoi.

Private car transfer to the airport for your flight to Da Lat. On arrival, private pick-up and transfer to your 3★ hotel for check-in. Evening at leisure to explore Da Lat; dinner at the hotel.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Brunch, Dinner
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Da Lat.
DAY 04

Lang Biang Land Full Day Tour

Da Lat

Breakfast at the hotel. Full-day tour from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM:

  • Visit Lang Biang Land — Da Lat's iconic mountain plateau
  • Explore the Fresh Garden — vibrant floral landscapes
  • Visit Que Garden with its themed displays
  • Enjoy the cool mountain air and panoramic Da Lat views

Return to your hotel in the evening; free time to explore Da Lat night market.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Da Lat.
DAY 05

Da Lat – Departure

Da Lat

Breakfast and check out. Private car transfer to Da Lat Airport for your departure flight.

End of services.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast
