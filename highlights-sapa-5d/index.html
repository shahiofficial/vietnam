Day by day

Your itinerary

DAY 01

Hanoi Arrival – City Discovery

Hanoi

Arrive at Noi Bai International Airport. Welcomed by your local guide and transferred to your 3★ hotel in the Old Quarter for check-in and rest.

Free time to explore the cultural heart of Hanoi:

  • Walk around legendary Hoan Kiem Lake
  • Discover the vibrant Old Quarter
  • Try Vietnamese coffee on the famous Train Street
🍽  Meals: None
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 02

Halong Bay Full Day – Sleeper Train to Sapa

Halong Bay → Sapa

Breakfast, check out and leave your large suitcase at the hotel. 08:00 — Pick-up and transfer to Halong Bay via the expressway (approx. 2.5 hours).

Halong Bay full-day cruise:

  • Sail through thousands of dramatic limestone karsts
  • Visit Sung Sot (Surprise) Cave
  • Kayaking or bamboo boat experience
  • Lunch served on board
  • Swimming and relaxing on the sundeck

Late afternoon — return transfer to Hanoi. A day-use room is provided for shower before the train. Evening — transfer to Hanoi Railway Station; 21:00–22:00 board the overnight sleeper train (soft berth, 4-pax A/C cabin) to Lao Cai.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Overnight on sleeper train.
DAY 03

Sapa Trekking – Ethnic Villages

Sapa / Muong Hoa Valley

Early-morning arrival at Lao Cai Railway Station. Transfer to Sapa by shared tourist bus. Early check-in subject to availability; otherwise a basic room is provided for shower. Breakfast at the hotel.

09:30 — Trek begins:

  • 6 km descent through Muong Hoa Valley
  • Visit Y Linh Ho — village of the Black H'mong people
  • 2 km trek down to Lao Chai through terraced rice fields
  • Continue to Ta Van village of the Dzay minority people
  • Lunch with a local family
  • Final 2 km trek to the bus stop, then return to Sapa by jeep or minivan

Trekking level: 6 / 10.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Sapa.
DAY 04

Fansipan Cable Car – Luxury Bus to Hanoi

Sapa → Hanoi

Breakfast at the hotel.

Morning — Fansipan Cable Car experience:

  • Transfer to Fansipan Cable Car Station
  • 20-minute cable car ride with stunning aerial views
  • Ascend to 2,800 metres — photo spots and pagoda visits
  • Walk up 600 steps to Fansipan's peak (approx. 1.5 hours)

Afternoon return to Sapa town; free time at Sapa Market. Evening — board the luxury sleeper cabin bus to Hanoi; late-night arrival and transfer to your 3★ hotel.

Trekking level: 4 / 10.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Overnight at 3★ hotel in Hanoi.
DAY 05

Hanoi – Departure

Hanoi

Breakfast, packing and check out. Free time until your private transfer to Noi Bai International Airport for your departure flight.

End of services.

🍽  Meals: Breakfast
